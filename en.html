ABOUT US

Quadro Systems Company delivers software development and related IT services for the enterprise market. The company was founded in 2011 by a group of seasoned IT professionals. The company's software development center is located in Tashkent, Uzbekistan.

In our work we are guided by the following principles, which help us to achieve our goals:

Partnering with clients

We make every effort to earn the trust of our customers and become their long-term partners. We carefully study each customer case to understand the client’s needs and objectives, and deliver a reliable and efficient solution. We always try to make our most, and even a little more for our partners as our success is tied to the success of our clients.

Professional ethics

We carry out all our obligations, comply with laws, ethical standards and business regulations.

Quality

The quality of our services is our reputation and hallmark. We do our utmost to ensure the highest quality of delivered solutions.

Sustainability

We care about the working conditions and professional growth of our employees. The company has established learning program that enables employees to constantly improve and enhance the quality of their work.

SERVICES

We provide a wide range of services from consulting to design, development and maintenance of software systems.

Software development

Quadro Systems provides a full range of software development services. We are working in close cooperation with our clients at all stages of the project - analysis of requirements and project planning, creation of design documentation, design and prototyping, development, testing, implementation and support of the final product. As a result of this cooperation our customers receive efficient and reliable software, which fully meets their needs and expectations.

Support & Maintenance

Our clients invest their efforts and funds in software solutions for their business problems. By providing technical support services for these solutions, we are helping to protect these investments. We do our best to ensure reliable and uninterrupted operation of the information systems of our clients, thus reducing the costs of ownership, increasing performance and ROI. To achieve these goals we perform continuous monitoring of products performance, timely solve emerging accidents, analyze the causes that may lead to these situations and optimize program code in order to prevent their occurrence in the future.

System integration

IT infrastructure of enterprise customers may consist of a variety of heterogeneous software solutions. Ensuring their interactions sometimes is not a trivial task. Quadro Systems offers its services for the integration of information systems for the various scenarios - integration at the backend level, integration at the data level, integration using web services or API.

Technology Consulting

The development of IT technologies has given rise to a huge range of platforms, products, methods and approaches for various business tasks. Companies that invest in appropriate technology gain competitive advantage and stimulate growth of their business. But how to make the right choice? Quadro Systems, based on its experience, knowledge and business process analysis provides consulting services to its customers on selection of optimal IT technologies and solutions.
